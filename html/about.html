On Writing

My musings usually start as fragments in Obsidian, then type them up here. Later, I circle back, change a word or a line (possibility becomes inevitability, that sort of thing).

These are like lived-in logs. Some wear the first draft proudly, some get reshaped quietly.
Every piece, in my head, has a musical pairing. Writing without music feels unfinished.

If for some reason, you didn't understand a piece, it's because it was never intended to be...
