Who am I? and What is this site about?

I’m Bala, a Bioscience graduate fixated on gene retention(or rather, the absence of it).

This site is meant to be a glimpse of my interests. A place to collect and explore ideas that matter to me. Obsidian was great(still is), but I wanted more control to how everything looked and felt.

So that's where this site comes in. A digital notebook for the long future ahead, where I can trace back my ideas, digital notes(not just yet, but someday), and projects. Heck, my entire digital footprint.

Sort of a Digital Garden, but it’s a work in progress, so expect changes.
