Who am I? and What is this site about?

Disclaimer: It is easy to ask who one is but rarely does one tries to look into it and put out a congolmeration of who one is. It is tedious; oblivion is much more safe or easy path for many. But not knowing thyself, What a waste!

I’m Bala, a Bioscience graduate fascinated by gene retention(or rather, the absence of it).

This site is meant to be a glimpse of my interests. A place to collect and explore ideas that matter to me. Obsidian was great(still is), but I wanted more control to how everything looked and felt.

So that's where this site comes in. A digital notebook for the long future ahead, where I can dump my ideas, content that I find amusing, digital notes(not just yet, but someday), and projects. Heck, my entire digital footprint.

But it’s a work in progress, so expect changes.
